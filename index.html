What is Munćej glosa?

Munćej glosa (language of the people) is a worldlang that is a posteriori constructed language using languages of the whole world as its source. Word "munćej" is adjective of word "munće" meaning human. Munće takes its origin from central african (where people are originally from) bantu word "muntu" - human and from Mapudungun word "che" - human, where people arrived at the latest period. This way word munće represents all groups of people. Below is world map divided by 15 sectors.

This worldlang borrows vocabulary from each indigenous language selected by black dot on the map. Each sector is meant to has 16 languages selected in order to be very representative as truly international languages. However later it may exceed this number of source languages. Munćej contains indigenous languages from all inhabitated by people continents including Australia. Borders of sectors has been drawn as compromise between distribution of language families and geopolitical boundaries.
