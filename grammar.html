Conjugation

Infinitive verbs are created simply by adding suffix -t for imperfective and -k for perfective verbs. Munćej has complex yet very consistent conjugation system. To create finite verbs simply add suffix to root noun according to table below. Note that letters r, l, w corresponds with pronouns ri (I), li (you) w (he/she), letter n corresponds with plurality, letters o, a, e corresponds with past, present and future tenses, letter t corresponds with infinitive aspect, letter k corresponds with finite aspect, letter f corresponds with subjunctive mood and letter h corresponds with imperative mood.
